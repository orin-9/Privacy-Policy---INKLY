Privacy Policy for INKLY

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

Welcome to INKLY. Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how our Chrome Extension handles data.

1. Data Collection

INKLY is designed to be a privacy-first tool. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). No email addresses, names, or browsing histories are ever sent to our servers.

2. Use of Permissions

To provide its core features, INKLY requires the following permissions:

  • contextMenus: To allow you to highlight text via the right-click menu.
  • storage: To save your highlights locally on your browser so they persist after a refresh.
  • activeTab: To apply the highlight colors to the page you are currently viewing.

3. Data Storage

All highlights created using INKLY are stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API. We have no access to your saved data.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties. Since no data is collected, there is no data to be shared.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support section.
